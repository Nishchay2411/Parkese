Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How does ParkEase work? ▼
Drivers search for available spots, book hourly slots, and navigate to the location. Owners list their private spots and earn money when they're not using them.
Is it free to sign up? ▼
Yes! Signing up as a driver or owner is completely free. Drivers pay only for the parking they book. Owners earn money with no upfront cost.
Can I extend my booking? ▼
Absolutely. From your driver dashboard, you can extend any active booking by additional hours, as long as the slot is available.
How do I list my parking spot? ▼
Register as an Owner, go to your dashboard, click "Add Spot", fill in the details (address, price, slots), and it goes live immediately.
What if I need to cancel? ▼
You can cancel a booking from your dashboard before the start time. Cancellation policies vary by spot owner.
